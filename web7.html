Copyright and fair use

Read Copyright Overview from Stanford. Review one of the Copyright, fair use media sources. I'd like to share "Copyright and Fair Use Animation". There are rules we have to know about fair use: it applies with schoolwork or education, news reporting, criticizing or commenting, comedy or parody.

Attribution

Attribution is giving credit to the creator of a copyright work. Since everything is automatically copyright, if you use it, you need to provide attribution. For academic publications, this is also know as citation. Find an article (or video) that provides information about attribution.

Digital footprint

Digital Footprint. Your social media activity, the info on your personal website, your browsing history, your online subscriptions, any photo galleries and videos you’ve uploaded — essentially, anything on the Internet with your name on it. Just like a tattoo, your digital reputation is an expression of yourself. It's highly visible, and hard to remove.
