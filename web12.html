My project

Site's goal

My project is a prototype of a social site for kids. They can share their stories, projects, pictures, pics, they can discuss some interesting news and play games. It is the place for bringing them together for fun as well as to teach them some social skills. Since my project didn't have a back-end part, it doesn't provide full functionality.
