ESL class

ESL stands for English as the Second Language. This is Advanced Reading and Vocabulary class. I took it because it develops thoughtful analysis and critical thinking as well as presentation and writing skills. We had very creative team project: we were expected to play some scene from the novel or to make a talk show with the novel's characters. Our teacher was amazing person because she was not only sharing her knowledge and emotions to the class but also her life's experiences, and some of her stories have changed my own understanding what was the role of teaching and interaction between a teacher and a student. Anyway the most interesting part of this class was our articles, and I'd like to share some of my summaries.

1. American Values

We discussed series of articles about American values and assumptions, cross-cultural differences in non-verbal communication, American attitude to time and punctuality.
