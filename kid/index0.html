Social skills for kids

We are social site for kids. They can share their stories, projects, pictures, photos, they can discuss some interesting news and games. It is a place for bringing them together for fun as well as to teach them some social skills.

We want to teach kids how to communivate online, to be respectful, to appreciate others efforts. Listening to others and being listened are the basic social demands for us all. We also will provide some educational content: puzzles, quizes, problems.

Creative use of computers

We'd like kids to use computers not only for video games, but also for new knowledge and for inspiration for some new projects. Kids are welcome to share new resources, new ideas how to create something interesting.
