26 Time Management Hacks I Wish I'd Known at 20. It is the presentation about effective time management. Among 26 hacks I choose 3 to share. The first one is: "we are always more focused and productive with limited time." So limited time is not a problem, but our life's factor that helps us to focus and to reach our goals. The author also invokes us to use constraints as opportunities. It is new point to me to look on my constraints as on opportunities :)

The other thing to share is: "Doing is better than perfect", It is Facebook company motto. It is about our expectations of perfect results that can kill desire to work. It is about choice: to find excuse why you can't do it or just to try.
