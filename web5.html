Explore the Wikimedia Commons

It is a database of more than 20 million freely usable media files to which anyone can contribute. Wikimedia Commons is part of the non-profit, multilingual, free-content Wikimedia family. Everyone is allowed to copy, use and modify any files here freely as long as they follow the terms specified by the author; this often means crediting the source and author(s) appropriately and releasing copies/improvements under the same freedom to others.
