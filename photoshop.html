Photoshop: before and after

This class was interesting and useful. You can use results of your learning in everyday life by editing photos, creating video clips, drawing pictures. These skills are necessary for a web developer. I do not use Photoshop, I use open sourced GIMP but the main tools for editing pictures are the same.
